About Us

Noor Makkah Center specialized in teaching Arabic to non-native speakers, as well as teaching the Holy Qur’an and Prophetic hadiths .

Noor Makkah Center was established in 2018 It aims to raise the standards of teaching Arabic to non-native speakers to a professional scientific level .

  • teaching the younger generation to recite the Qur’an
  • specialized in teaching Arabic only to non-native speakers
  • supervised by experts in the Arabic language with extensive experience of years in the field of teaching

we rely on the modern, eloquent Arabic language that is close to the spoken language, as it is the language that is understood throughout the Arab world,

Why Choose Noor Makkah center ?

Noor Makkah Center relies on contemporary Arabic as the language of instruction while not neglecting the local dialects, which makes it easier for the learner to communicate with the largest possible segment of Arabic speakers.

The message:

To highlight the beauty of the Arabic tongue, the splendor of its statement, and the pleasure of learning and teaching it (research and application).

Vision:

Developing the process of teaching Arabic to non-native speakers in all its elements through teacher training, preparing and developing modern educational curricula, and making use of educational means and modern technologies.

Objectives:

To provide effective programs and courses that cover the various interests of learners. Preparing curricula for teaching Arabic to non-native speakers of different specializations and inclinations.
